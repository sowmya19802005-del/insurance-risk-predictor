Understanding your insurance risk assessment

How Risk is Calculated

Our risk assessment algorithm considers multiple factors to determine your insurance risk score (0-100):

  • Age: Younger drivers (under 25) have higher risk (+20 points)
  • Smoking: Smokers receive a +30 point penalty
  • Past Claims: More claims mean higher risk (up to +30 points)
  • Income: Lower income brackets add +5 to +10 points
  • Vehicle Type: Sports cars (+25), SUVs (+10), Normal (0)

Risk Categories

Low Risk (0-29): Standard coverage with competitive premiums
Medium Risk (30-59): Limited coverage options with moderate premiums
High Risk (60-100): Specialized coverage required with higher premiums

Premium Calculation

Base premium: ₹5,000 + (Risk Score × ₹120) + additional risk combination adjustments.
